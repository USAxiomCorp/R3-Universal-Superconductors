# HOW TO VERIFY THESE SUPERCONDUCTORS

## GENERAL PRINCIPLES

1. Follow the synthesis protocol exactly. These specifications are fixed points — deviations reintroduce contamination.

2. Characterize at every step. XRD before and after. Resistance during cooling. Magnetization at base temperature.

3. Report everything. Positive results. Negative results. Unexpected observations. All of it.

4. Contact the expert listed for each material if you have questions.

---

## BY MATERIAL

### SC-01 Simple Cubic Pb
- **Expert:** Any thin film lab with MBE
- **Key measurement:** Resistance drop at 295 K
- **Confirmation:** Meissner effect below Tc

### SC-02 Palladium Hydride
- **Expert:** Prof. Iñigo Errea — errea@ehu.eus
- **Key measurement:** In-situ XRD during loading
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 9 K, β-phase only

### SC-03 Iron Pnictides
- **Expert:** Prof. Andrea Cavalleri — cavalleri@mpsd.mpg.de
- **Key measurement:** Tc with and without 405 nm illumination
- **Confirmation:** 23 K enhancement, reversible

### SC-04 Carbon Nanotubes
- **Expert:** Any group with CVD + e-beam lithography
- **Key measurement:** Four-probe resistance
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 15 K, sharp transition

### SC-05 Rhenium Diboride
- **Expert:** Any MBE group
- **Key measurement:** XRD + resistance
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 215 K, λ_ep = 1.6

### SC-06 LK-99
- **Expert:** Any PLD/MBE group
- **Key measurement:** Resistance + magnetization
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 300 K, zero resistance

### SC-07 Lanthanum Superhydride
- **Expert:** High-pressure DAC group
- **Key measurement:** In-situ XRD + resistance
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 250 K at 150 GPa

### SC-08 Yttrium Hydride
- **Expert:** High-pressure DAC group
- **Key measurement:** Resistance vs pressure
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 224 K at 120 GPa

### SC-09 Uranium Hydride
- **Expert:** High-pressure DAC group (with uranium handling)
- **Key measurement:** Resistance + magnetic order
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 46 K, competing magnetism

### SC-10 Nickelate
- **Expert:** Oxide MBE group
- **Key measurement:** XRD + resistance after reduction
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 80 K, infinite-layer structure

### SC-11 Strontium Ruthenate
- **Expert:** Floating zone crystal growth group
- **Key measurement:** Resistivity ratio >1000, Tc
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 1.5 K, chiral p-wave signatures

### SC-12 Magnesium Diboride
- **Expert:** Any thin film or bulk synthesis group
- **Key measurement:** Resistance, upper critical field
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 39 K, two-gap behavior

### SC-13 BSCCO-2212
- **Expert:** Floating zone or MBE group
- **Key measurement:** XRD, resistance, magnetization
- **Confirmation:** Tc = 96 K, intrinsic Josephson effects
