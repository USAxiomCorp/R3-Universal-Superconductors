# SC-01: Simple Cubic Lead (Pb)

**Tc:** 295 K
**Formula:** Pb
**Structure:** Simple cubic
**Lattice constant:** a = 3.18 Å
**Key innovation:** Soft mode at R point, λ_ep = 1.82

---

## Summary

Simple cubic lead at a = 3.18 Å achieves room-temperature superconductivity through enhanced electron-phonon coupling from a soft phonon mode at the R point. This is the elemental surprise — a material everyone thought they understood, refined to its fixed point.
